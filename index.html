About




Hi! My name is Haitham Awad, I am a first generation Palestinian-American born and raised in New Jersey. I developed my love for coding and creating after participating in the All Star Code Intensive Program the summer after my sophomore year of high school. Shortly after completing it, I decided to build this website to keep track of all of my projects and passions. Happy browsing!

Projects

Pong

Who doesn’t love a little stress free gaming? For this project, completed during All Star Code, my group and I created Pong using P5 in JavaScript. It simulates the classic arcade game from many years ago, giving the user a nostalgic feel. The gameplay is simple: two players try to block the bouncing ball from going past their bar that they move up and down. Whichever player lets the ball pass their bar first loses.

Watch Site

For this project I transformed a bland and boring website into this sleek watch viewing experience. In order to achieve this by using design skills to move different aspects of the website around, changing fonts, centering texts, and adding colors.
